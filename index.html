Raptors of Berlin

Like any megapolis, Berlin is a true jungle. Important distinction: not a concrete one, but jungle with trees and wild animals. Or, for example, birds of prey.

Why do wild birds love Berlin?

Greenery

18% of Berlin are forests. 12% more are parks and gardens, including cemeteries. Raptors do have place to live.

Flat area

The highest hill in Berlin is slightly less than 121m tall (Arkenberg). The flatland makes perfect hunting grounds.

All the food

Raptors are doing quite well in terms of food here. Their menu comprises rodents, small birds, and wild rabbits.
